About Smartphone Management

Welcome to the Smartphone Management System! This system is designed to help you manage information about various smartphones. Whether you are keeping track of inventory, prices, or specifications, our system provides a user-friendly interface for efficient smartphone management.

Features of our system include:

Adding new smartphones with details like brand, model, storage, and price.

Viewing a list of smartphones with their key specifications.

Easy updating and removal of smartphone entries.

Feel free to explore the other pages of our system to add, view, update, and remove smartphones as needed. If you have any questions or feedback, please contact our support team.
