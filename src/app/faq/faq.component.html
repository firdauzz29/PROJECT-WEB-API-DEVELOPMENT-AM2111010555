FAQ - Smartphone Management System

How do I add a new smartphone?
To add a new smartphone, go to the "Add Smartphone" page and fill in the required details in the form.
Can I update the information of an existing smartphone?
Yes, you can update the information of an existing smartphone on the "Update Smartphone" page by providing the new details.
How do I remove a smartphone from the system?
To remove a smartphone, go to the "View Smartphones" page, find the smartphone you want to remove, and click the "Remove" button.
